Anonymous

Hacktivist Collective (Est. ~2003)

Who They Are

Anonymous is a decentralized international hacktivist collective that is widely known for its various cyber attacks against several governments, government institutions and government agencies, corporations, and the Church of Scientology.

Anonymous originated in 2003 on the imageboard 4chan, representing the concept of many online and offline community users simultaneously existing as an anarchic, digitized global brain. They are known for their use of the Guy Fawkes mask as a symbol, popularized by the graphic novel and film "V for Vendetta."

Notable Operations

  • Operation Chanology (2008) - Protest campaign against the practices of the Church of Scientology
  • Operation Payback (2010) - Attacks on anti-piracy organizations and financial institutions that refused to process donations to WikiLeaks
  • Operation Tunisia/Egypt (2011) - Support for the Arab Spring protests
  • Operation AntiSec (2011) - Targeted law enforcement agencies and major corporations
  • Operation Hong Kong (2019) - Support for the Hong Kong protests
  • Operations against Russian governmental websites (2022) - Following the Russian invasion of Ukraine

Their Message

"We are Anonymous. We are Legion. We do not forgive. We do not forget. Expect us."
